Education is so important. Let me tell you a few things about skincare...

People that came into my office almost universally had the same 2 questions How much will this cost me and how long will it take. Unfortunately those are not ever questions even the most experienced professionals can answer. Your skin is alive and it will react differently than other people's. The standard for someone who is rigorous with their routine can expect good results and a change in weeks, but hitting the goal of healthy skin can be a journey that takes months.
