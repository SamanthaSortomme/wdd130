Welcome to Sbubbles! I hope you will find what you are looking for. We have education and products on other pages, you will find the links on the bottom left of this page. Feel free to ask any questions.

People will come to me because they are recommended to and I will get recommendations by being honest. There is so much that happens behind the scenes and I want to break down that wall and bring transparency into the industry. Not just for clients either. I have spent thousands of dollars over the years to learn what I know. Most of that money was wasted. I would love to spare fellow aestheticians the heartache I have gone through. All of the money I made from my spa went right back into education and products for my practice to make me better. I wish someone had told me what works and what doesn't so I didn't have to fall for every lie I did. I was finally in a place where I was able to pay my rent and not need more classes when I was shut down. This was such a big blow to me. For years I did things wrong, and now I have all of this knowledge that could really help people. I even had people in my Pathways class that would reach out for advice. By having open communication I can post new topics that come up and after a while it would be fun to add in a “I've never seen that” section for weird things. It would be great to have my clients have a voice on there too. Put quotes as well as reviews. Everyone likes to see a lot of good reviews, but it would add to the fun atmosphere to have conversation snippets too.

People will come to this site to be educated in skincare and see options available to them. They will be able to see what Sbubbles Spa is all about and also get information on products. I think one of the pages will be for products and I'm not sure about the other one. Maybe education and false things that a lot of people believe. I think it would be nice to have photos of skin, products, and services. I owned a spa before Covid and I would like to open another spa after we move so this is a great basis for what a website could look like for me in a few years when I'm ready. I think the thing people really need is accessible information, even routines. I had a lot of people call and text me because they lost their instructions and even more that would get home and do things wrong. Products need to be put on in a specific order and if I had that order online they could look it up anytime. That convenience would really help people stay on track for reaching their goals. I could also have tips and tricks for people who are not interested in products or treatments, but are on a skin care website so they are clearly interested. Closing ideas Overall I'm really excited. I know this won't be my website, but I love skincare and the idea of putting together a site for something I'm passionate about is really exciting. I love the colors! I had such a fun time picking colors and a theme for my spa and I know doing it for my website will be fun too! Who will come to your site and why People that come to my site will be interested in skincare. They will expect me to answer questions like how much will this cost me and how long will it take. Unfortunately those are not ever questions we can answer. I will have to explain that skin is alive and it reacts differently with each person. The standard for someone who is rigorous with their routine can expect good results in less than a month, but hitting the goal of healthy skin can be a journey. What activities will be available The activities on my site will be education as well as feeling good. A spa site should make someone feel happy to be on. I should have clients that just like hopping on because it makes them feel good. I will also post new facts and things learned to keep my page relevant. I want more traffic so I need to keep people coming back. There will be no information necessary, I will not take payments or book appointments online.
